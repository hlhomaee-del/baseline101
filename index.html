Premium CCMA Exam Prep Platform

Teach it. Practice it. Test it. Reflect.

This single HTML app turns your 90-question CCMA baseline into a colorful clinical reasoning experience. Students build concepts first, practice with rationales, enter timed challenge mode, take an exam simulation, and review weak areas.

Guided Practice
